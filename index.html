About Me

I have drawn all of my life. When I set off to learn computer science at UC Santa Cruz, my experiences drawing allowed me to develop an artistic approach to software engineering. In my development process, I treat problems like a muse, observing them meticulosouly for inspiration. In a cylce, I make an examination then add a (key)stroke, letting each change guide and mold the design of the solution over the course of its implemetation.

Just like with drawing, I fell in love with my craft and learned to enjoy all of the technical challenges between starting from scratch and developing a fully functional program. My passion inspires me to continuously learn and grow.

Recently, I undertook App Academy's coding bootcamp to nurture my web development skills. Over 16, 100-hour weeks, I learned to create single-page applications from the front-to the back-end using trending technologies like JavaScript, React, Redux, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3, Node, Ruby, Rails, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Mongoose, and Webpack. (Check out my projects)

I am also interested in fostering my leadership skills and learning how to drive software teams to reach their optimal performance levels. I want to lead projects that positively impact communities through a commitment to product quality.

If you haven't guessed already, I explore art on my free time. It began with pencil drawings but it's extended to reading and journaling as of recently. I read books I find peace and excitement in taking a journey through my thoughts.
