About Me

From when I began my career as a software engineer at UCSC to when I graduated with a B.S. in computer science, my life experiences with art influenced my approach in developing software. My method involves cycling between meticulously observing problems like muses and letting each examination reshape the design of a solution over the course of its implementation.

Just like with drawing, I fell in love with my craft and learned to enjoy all of the technical challenges between starting from scratch and developing a fully functional program. My passion inspires me to continuously learn and grow.

I am interested in fostering my leadership skills and learning how to drive software teams to reach their optimal performance levels. I want to lead projects that positively impact communities through a commitment to product quality.

I began to explore art with pencil drawings but it's extended to reading and journaling as of recently. On my free time, you can find me studying psychology, computer science, or writing. I find peace and excitement in taking a journey through my thoughts.
