About Me

I love to draw. In my university education, this inclined me to consider software engineering as an art and the problems it solves, muses. From then on, I have been inspired to explore and capture the most challenging muses.

I am interested in learning how to drive software teams to reach their optimal performance levels. I want to lead them in projects that positively impact the communities our software serves through a commitment to product quality.

If you haven't guessed already, I explore art on my free time. It began with pencil drawings, but as of recently, it's extended to reading and writing. I find peace and excitement in taking a journey through my thoughts.
